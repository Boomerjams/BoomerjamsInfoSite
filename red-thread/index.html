About Red Thread

Red Thread is a dating app in development that allows users to be matched BY one another, allowing for less stress, information overflow, endless swiping, and more honest connections, with input from experienced people from all sorts of walks of life.

Focused on compatibility, speed, and satisfaction rather than endless swiping, users are matched by one another instead of swiping for themselves.

Status

  • Status: Private build in progress.
  • Focus: Dating with better results and less strain.
  • Release: Not yet published.

Everyone is welcome on the waitlist

The waitlist isn't just for daters. We're onboarding three different kinds of early users, so anyone interested can join — there's a role for you regardless of where you are in life.

  • Daters — people who want to be matched and meet someone genuinely.
  • Judges — people who help match others, sharing their perspective and experience.
  • Testers — people who just want to try the app early and share feedback.

If any of that sounds like you, hop on the list. We'd love to have you.
